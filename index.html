Dwait Bhatt

I am a graduate researcher at the Existential Robotics Laboratory at UC San Diego, where I work on robot manipulation and reinforcement learning. Previously, I was a Machine Learning Engineer at Samsung Research for 3 years.

My research goal is to develop a reward design framework that ensures safe and aligned reinforcement learning agents. Always open to talk about AI safety, robotics, and machine learning. Feel free to reach out over mail.
